In the summer of 2019, I began to learn how to code.

In the middle of June 2019, I decided I wanted to be GOOD-no, GREAT at coding. I've picked up coding before in January and bought the course made by Andrew Mead called The Modern Javascript Bootcamp (2019), but I never really touched it after the first couple of sections covering basic syntax. I thought I didn't have enough time and brainpower, so I wasn't committed to it.However, I now realize that coding isn't something you can just learn in a couple of weeks, but it isn't rocket science either. So from the midst of June til now (july 3rd), I've learned how to become discplined, committed, and driven to code I feel like I've found my passion in web development.

To code is to learn how to think. Thoughts are all we have. I think it's only fitting that my blog will mostly be a documentation of my journey, values, and triumphs.

I'm eager to share this series of my thoughts, so follow along with me!
