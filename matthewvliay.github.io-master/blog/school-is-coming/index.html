School is coming

..so I built a quiz app!

Now that school is here .. I've made a quiz app for upcoming freshmen at my school! I thought about making this ever since the beginning of August, but I was holding it off for a couple of weeks because of my procrastination habits lol but I've finally made it! :)

It's an elementary quiz app, so no need to explain the rundown in depth. Essentially, there's a landing screen with a button to start the quiz, and you're given a question with three options, and you're given 10 seconds to answer for each question. That's it There's also a question timer score container, but they are just accessories.

Live demo pictures: You can view the source code Here

There are only three questions. They are general, plan, and simple for now, so earlier today(8.18) I made a Facebook post asking for questions to make for the approaching freshmen(or upperclassmen who need a refresher).
